Two Simultaneous Movements

Two Simultaneous Movements is a sound art installation set in a room divided in two by a black line down the centre. Either side of the room is two plinths with two objects/sound sources playing music. On the left hand plinth is a motorised programable music box playing a piece of music from, on loop, at a medium to slow tempo. On the right hand plinth is a laptop playing a recording of the motorised programable music box performing the same piece of music but at a slightly faster tempo so as to make the two sound sources play slightly out of sync with each other, this is a compositional choice. My intention is to create an environment that allows the participant to meditate on the differences between the objects whilst immersed in their performance; how do you excercise control?
