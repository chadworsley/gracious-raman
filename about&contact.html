Chad Worsley

Creative Musician/Sonic Artist





I’ve been involved in music since the age of 9 when I first picked up instruments. For the majority of time since my main interest has been composition, production and performance of songs but over the last couple of years I’ve branched out into experimenting with spatial sound and audio-based art installations. I'm currently particularly interested in experimenting with spatial sound technology and also exploring how music can be used as a mode of expression in different mediums. I have an undergraduate degree in Music and Sound Production which concentrated on composition and production of music for visual media.
